About me

Hello My name is Amirhossein SadeghiNiya, but I like to call myself Am1rHus.I was born in 2004.My first experience working with my computer goes back to when I was 4 or 5 years old when I was browsing the internet and playing games with my aunt's son's computer. When I was 7 years old, after my father's death, my family bought me a computer, and this was the beginning of my story with computers and technology. I chose network and software (computer) in high school. In my first year of high school, they taught us Algorithms and Flowcharts, Basic C# Sharp, Network Plus, etc. and C# became the first programming language I learned. In the second year, our work was mostly with the web and teaching us PHP, MySQL, HTML, CSS, JS, Bootstrap, etc., but my main interest was PHP, and I continued it In the third year, they taught us security, income generation, marketing, etc

I went to university in 2022. I am studying computer engineering. I hope to be able to migrate to another country in the next few years and continue my career there better and more easily.
